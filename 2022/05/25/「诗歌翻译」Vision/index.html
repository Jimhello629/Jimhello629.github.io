「诗歌翻译」Vision

「诗歌翻译」Vision - May Theilgaard Watts

Vision (Original)

To-day there have been lovely things
I never saw before;
Sunlight through a jar of marmalade;
A blue gate;
A rainbow
In soapsuds on dishwater;
Candlelight on butter;
The crinkled smile of a little girl
Who had new shoes with tassels;
A chickadee on a thorn-apple;
Empurpled mud under a willow,
Where white geese slept;
White ruffled curtains sifting moonlight
On the scrubbed kitchen floor;
The under side of a white-oak leaf;
Ruts in the road at sunset;
An egg yolk in a blue bowl.
My love kissed my eyes last night.

目之所及（译）

今日目之所及均是可爱之物，
过往竟未有幸一遇；
斜阳穿过盛满果酱的玻璃罐；
穿堂内幽蓝隐隐；
一抹彩虹，
浮在满是肥皂泡的洗碗水上：
黄油块上烛光摇曳；
小女孩笑眯了眼，
脚踩流苏盈盈的新鞋；
山雀立于曼陀罗花枝；
紫黑的土壤上柳丝飘飘，
白鹅正在地上匍匐酣睡；
月光筛过流动的布帘，
洒落在刚擦净的厨房地板；
白橡树叶背过头去；
落日余晖映出过往的车辙；
一颗蛋黄静躺在蓝瓷碗中；
是你，我的爱人，
昨夜在我眼眸种下一吻。

About May Theilgaard Watts

May Petrea Theilgaard Watts (1 May 1893 – 20 August 1975) was an American naturalist, writer, poet, illustrator, and educator. She was a naturalist at The Morton Arboretum and author of Reading the Landscape of America.
